The Haunting of Hill House

Genre: Horror, Drama
A modern reimagining of Shirley Jackson's classic novel, this series follows a family confronting haunting memories of their old home and the terrifying events that drove them from it.

Review 1: “A terrifyingly beautiful story that stays with you long after watching. The character arcs are incredible.”

Review 2: “Well-paced and genuinely frightening! A must-watch for horror fans.”

Review 3: “Some parts felt drawn out, but the atmosphere is hauntingly effective.”
